The customer book as it is — lifetime revenue, currently-open AR, time-to-pay distribution. Descriptive view; SFS bids every job and can't force payment timing.
How to read this page: nothing here is "do something" — it's "here's the shape of the book." TX subcontractors have no enforceable payment deadlines, so days-out is just how long customers historically take to pay, not a problem list. Concentration % is informational context for SBA loan narratives, not a warning.
