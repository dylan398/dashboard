Forecasts
Unified financial forecast — AR collections, pipeline-to-revenue, and 12-month projection. Planning inputs, not alarms.
How the three forecasts interact:
• AR forecast tracks already-invoiced money — for each open invoice, expected pay date = invoice date + that customer's median DSO (auto-computed from QBO Transaction Detail).
• Pipeline forecast turns Knowify pending bids into expected new revenue — pending CV × historical $-win rate. Then lagged by bid-to-revenue median (when bids actually convert to invoices) + portfolio DSO (when invoices become cash).
• Financial projection extrapolates next-12-month revenue from last complete year's monthly shape × current YoY pace. Holds OpEx at last-year monthly average and gross margin at last-year actual.
The combined cash-inflow forecast adds AR + pipeline expected cash by 30/60/90/120-day bucket. Pipeline lands later because of the bid→invoice→cash chain.
Need at least Open Invoices, Knowify, and one complete year of P&L to render forecasts.
